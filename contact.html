Frequently Asked

We recommend smart casual to formal attire. Think linen shirts, dresses, chinos — clothes that match the elevated atmosphere. We gently decline entry to guests in beachwear, athletic clothing, or flip-flops.

We recommend booking 2–3 days in advance for weekday evenings, and 4–7 days ahead for Friday and Saturday dinners. For the Golden Hour slots (6:30–8:30 PM), we fill up quickly — book as early as possible. Same-day availability can be confirmed via WhatsApp.

Absolutely. We accommodate vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, Jain, and other dietary requirements. Please mention this in the Special Requests field when booking, or contact us directly. Our kitchen team will ensure your meal is crafted to suit your needs.

Cancellations made 24 hours before your reservation incur no charge. Cancellations within 24 hours may attract a per-person cancellation fee. For private events with a deposit, please refer to your booking agreement. We always try to accommodate rescheduling where possible.

Yes — our rooftop space is perfect for private birthdays, anniversaries, proposals, corporate dinners, and full buyouts. We offer customised menus, dedicated service staff, floral and décor arrangements, and live music coordination. Contact us at events@uprimanzil.com to begin planning.

Complimentary valet parking is available for all dinner guests. Please let us know on arrival. Street parking is also available nearby for lunch service.

Our rooftop is partially covered and we maintain a beautifully designed indoor overflow space with the same ambiance, lighting, and service. In severe weather conditions, we will contact you in advance to offer alternatives or reschedule.

We maintain a curated selection of Indian and imported wines, premium spirits, and craft cocktails. Our sommelier is available Thursday through Sunday to guide your pairing choices. The full beverage menu is available on our Menu page.
