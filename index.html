Our Philosophy

01

The Upper Floor.
The Elevated Experience.

"Upri Manzil" — a name that carries the spirit of elevation in every sense. We are not merely a restaurant perched above the city. We are a sanctuary where the sky becomes your ceiling, the horizon your backdrop, and every meal an act of deliberate luxury.

Our philosophy is simple: bring the finest ingredients together with artisanal craft, frame them within an atmosphere of warm twilight, and let the rooftop do the rest.

02 · The Experience

A ritual of light, flavour, and the open sky

Every evening between 6:30 PM and 8:30 PM, the rooftop reaches its peak beauty. We have designed an entire experience around this sacred window of light — welcome cocktails, a five-course tasting, and the sky doing what it does best.
